The BreadWinner

The Breadwinner is a 2017 animated film set in Taliban-controlled Afghanistan, focusing on an 11-year-old girl named Parvana. After her father is unjustly imprisoned, Parvana disguises herself as a boy to provide for her family, since women and girls are forbidden from going out alone. She teams up with a friend, Shauzia, who is also passing as a boy, and together they take on difficult jobs to survive. The film powerfully explores courage and resilience in the face of oppression, with Parvana using storytelling as a means to inspire hope.
